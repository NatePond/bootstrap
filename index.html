About Me!

Hello! My name is Nate Pond, a young professional, who is currently studying coding at UNC. My background is in community relations from my experience as a Community Relations Specialist for FEMA to my experience as a Field Manager during the election for Working America AFL-CIO.

I have a large variety of interests on topics raning from skepticism, ethics in journalism, Philosophy, Cyberpunk, Weird fiction, to Horror. I play guitar. I enjoy Munchkins.

Someday I would like to combine my knowledge of the anthropology of economics, media theory, and coding for the purpose of social engineering and facilitating localization. I'm also a practitioner of 'Pataphysics!
